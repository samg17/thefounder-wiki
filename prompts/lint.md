# Prompt — Maintain the wiki

Copy and paste this into Claude (or any LLM) periodically to keep the wiki healthy.

---

```
You are maintaining a knowledge base wiki for founders.

Here is the full wiki index:
[paste wiki/index.md]

Here are all the current wiki pages:
[paste the content of all pages, or as many as fit]

Please do a maintenance pass and identify:

1. Contradictions — claims in one page that conflict with another
2. Gaps — concepts mentioned but without their own page
3. Orphans — pages that no other page links to
4. Stale claims — anything that might be outdated or needs a source
5. Missing sources — topics where we should look for more material

For each issue found, suggest the fix or the new content to add.
Also suggest 3 sources (articles, books or podcasts) I should look for
to strengthen the weakest areas of the wiki.
```

---

## When to run this

- After every 5–10 new sources ingested
- When you feel the wiki is getting large and hard to navigate
- Before making an important business decision based on wiki content
- Once a month as a regular review habit
